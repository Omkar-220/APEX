🌊 Background Animations LIVE

  • 15–22s staggered keyframe cycles
  • Respects `prefers-reduced-motion`

💡 Inspiration & Design Notes

Typography Inter/system-ui. 16px base, 1.6 line-height. High x-height for readability during long sessions.
Spacing & Rhythm 4px baseline grid. Generous padding reduces cognitive load. Cards float with `backdrop-filter`.
Motion Rules Only `transform` & `opacity`. 0.2–0.4s ease curves. No parallax, no bounce. Purpose: calm focus.
Accessibility Contrast ≥ 4.5:1. `prefers-reduced-motion` honored. Focus states visible. Keyboard navigable.
